## Model

The users table contains people, while access lifecycle fields determine who can sign in. Assignment-only people have an active person status and no application access.

## Provisioning paths

| Path | Creates or updates | Grants sign-in |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Local People UI | Person profile | No |
| Admin grant access/invitation | Existing person access | Yes |
| SSO just-in-time behavior | Authenticated identity according to policy | According to policy |
| Application-access SCIM | Profile and access lifecycle | Yes |
| People-directory SCIM | Workforce profile and organization data | No |

## Authority

Track profile, access, and credential authority separately. Identity-provider-managed password and profile fields must remain read-only in Veviad.

## Operational checks

1. Provision one person through the intended path.
2. Verify department, location, status, and external identity.
3. For assignment-only scope, confirm the person appears in People but cannot sign in.
4. For access scope, confirm active access, role mapping, and SSO or invitation.
5. Deactivate and reactivate the test identity.
6. Confirm asset assignments and audit history remain intact.

## Safety

Use separate SCIM applications, tokens, and groups for access and People purposes. Token regeneration invalidates the previous token.
