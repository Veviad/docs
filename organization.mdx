Organization groups everything that shapes the workspace: business taxonomy, authentication, permissions, and integrations. Split between Settings (operational configuration) and Admin (access and identity).

## Highlights

- Sixteen settings areas for currency, depreciation, taxonomy, and more
- Roles, users, invitations, SSO, and SCIM under Admin
- Veviad AI provider configuration through Admin → Integrations
