## Purpose

Handovers provide evidence that a recipient accepted an assignment, return, or other custody change.

![Asset handover status and acknowledgement history](/images/screenshots/asset-handover.webp)

## Before you begin

You need handover permission, an asset, and a recipient. Organization defaults determine channels, PDF inclusion, and whether an external link is allowed.

## Request an acknowledgement

1. Open the asset and choose the handover or custody action.
2. Confirm the recipient, status or assignment change, and acknowledgement type.
3. Choose permitted delivery channels and PDF behavior.
4. Send the request.
5. Monitor pending, confirmed, disputed, or expired state.
6. Resolve disputes on the source asset and create a corrected request when necessary.

## Expected result

Veviad stores the request, immutable asset snapshot, delivery settings, confirmation identity, and timestamp.

## Best practices

- Use account-required acknowledgements when the recipient can sign in.
- Use external links only when permitted and necessary.
- Do not treat email delivery alone as confirmation.
- Keep the asset assignment accurate before requesting acknowledgement.

## Troubleshooting

If the recipient cannot confirm, check access mode, link expiry, account access, and mail delivery. Never copy a live external token into a support ticket.
