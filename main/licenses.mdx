## Purpose

License records track the entitlement and its available seats; they are separate from installed assets and accessories.

![Industrial software license inventory](/images/screenshots/licenses.webp)

## Before you begin

You need License view permission. Create, edit, delete, and seat assignment actions have separate permissions.

## Manage a license

1. Open **Licenses** and select a record.
2. Record product name, serial or contract identifier, licensed organization, purchase details, and validity dates.
3. Set the seat count and whether seats can be reassigned.
4. Assign seats to people or assets as supported by the entitlement.
5. Review expiration and maintenance status.

## Expected result

Veviad calculates available seats from the entitlement and its assignments, while the audit history records changes.

## Best practices

- Never place a real secret license key in screenshots or support tickets.
- Use one record per entitlement or contract boundary.
- Record renewal and termination dates separately.
- Use Reports for upcoming expirations and utilization.

## Troubleshooting

If no seat is available, check assignments and the total seat count. If a person is missing, verify the People directory rather than granting application access.
