Checklists standardize repeatable work on assets, such as commissioning, inspections, and decommissioning.

## Before you begin

You need permission to view the asset. Creating or completing checklist work also requires the corresponding checklist permission.

## Add and complete a checklist

1. Open **Assets** and select the asset.
2. Open the **Checklists** section.
3. Attach an active checklist template.
4. Complete items as the work is performed and add notes where evidence matters.
5. Review the progress and completed timestamp.

## Expected result

The checklist instance remains attached to the asset with a template snapshot, item progress, responsible users, and completion history.

<Note>
  Current checklists are asset-only. They are not attached to maintenance records, people, licenses, or accessories.
</Note>

## Best practices

- Use templates for stable procedures, not one-off notes.
- Mark safety-critical items as critical in Settings.
- Update a template for future instances; existing instances retain their snapshot.

## Troubleshooting

If the checklist action is missing, check your permission and confirm the template is active. An automation may also have created the instance already.
