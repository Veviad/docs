## Purpose

Accessories track stock quantities for supporting items that do not need an individual asset lifecycle.

![Machinery accessories and maintenance stock](/images/screenshots/accessories.webp)

## Before you begin

You need Accessory view permission. Creating, editing, deleting, and checking out stock require separate permissions.

## Manage accessory stock

1. Open **Accessories** and select an item.
2. Record model, quantity, minimum quantity, location, supplier, manufacturer, and unit cost.
3. Mark the item requestable when users may request it.
4. Record checkouts and returns against the appropriate person.
5. Review available quantity and replenish before the minimum threshold.

## Expected result

Available stock reflects the total quantity less open checkouts.

## Best practices

- Use an Asset instead when each unit needs its own tag, serial, maintenance, or handover.
- Use clear pack sizes and unit names.
- Keep minimum quantities aligned with lead times.
- Consolidate duplicates before reporting.

## Troubleshooting

If available quantity looks wrong, review open checkouts and returned quantities. A deleted or inactive person should not erase checkout history.
