# Turn everyday asset work into assisted work.

Veviad AI is positioned as the natural-language and smart-intake layer of the platform. It helps users find information faster, analyze data with less friction, and reduce manual entry where document-based intake is available.

## Highlights

- Natural-language interaction with workspace data
- Faster search and explanation workflows
- PDF-based asset extraction and review support
