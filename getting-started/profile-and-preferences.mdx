Your profile controls how Veviad presents dates, language, and notifications to you.

![Profile language and timezone preferences](/images/screenshots/profile-preferences.webp)

## Before you begin

You need an active Veviad account. Identity-provider-managed fields may be read-only.

## Update your preferences

1. Open your avatar menu and select **Profile**.
2. Update your name or contact details when those fields are managed locally.
3. Choose your **Language** and **Timezone**.
4. Open **Notification preferences** and enable only the events you need.
5. Save each section.

![Personal notification preferences](/images/screenshots/notification-preferences.webp)

## Expected result

The interface uses your language, timestamps render in your timezone, and notification delivery follows your selections. Schedules keep their own configured timezone.

## Best practices

- Use your primary working timezone, including daylight-saving rules.
- Keep security and handover notifications enabled.
- Ask an administrator when SSO or SCIM locks a profile field.

## Troubleshooting

If a change does not appear, reload once. A disabled field is usually controlled by the identity provider, not a browser error.
