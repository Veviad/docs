# Your First Workspace

The quickest way to make Veviad useful is to set up the shared structure before importing or assigning a large number of records.

## Recommended setup order

1. Confirm your users, roles, and departments.
2. Define locations, status labels, categories, and suppliers.
3. Review cost centers, depreciation rules, and asset tag settings.
4. Create one or two checklist templates for repeatable workflows.
5. Decide whether reports, notifications, and SSO should be configured immediately.

## Why structure first?

Assets become easier to search, report on, and govern when the underlying taxonomy is stable. Early structure pays off in:

- cleaner imports
- consistent assignment
- more meaningful reports
- better automation and checklist behavior

## Minimum setup checklist

- departments and locations reflect the real organization
- categories and status labels match how your team works
- suppliers and manufacturers are available for new records
- invitation flow is ready for the first operational users
- support links and documentation access are known internally
