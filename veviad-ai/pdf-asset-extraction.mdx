PDF Asset Extraction is an optional Veviad AI workflow for turning invoice or supplier PDFs into draft asset records. It is designed to reduce manual entry while keeping a human review step in place.

## Availability

The extraction action appears in **Assets** only when Veviad AI is enabled and an AI provider is configured.

![The Integrations page showing Veviad AI and Single-Sign-On status](/images/screenshots/admin-integrations.webp)

_View shown: `demo.veviad.cloud/admin/integrations`_

If the action is not visible, ask an administrator to open **Organization → Admin → Integrations**, enable **Veviad AI**, and configure the provider. The demo environment may show Veviad AI as inactive until that setup is completed.

## Workflow at a glance

1. Open **Assets** and choose the AI PDF extraction action when it is available.
2. Upload a supported PDF document, such as an invoice, purchase order, or packing list.
3. Allow the extraction process to read and classify the content.
4. Review extracted values in the guided interface.
5. Correct or enrich the results where needed.
6. Create the resulting asset records.

## What makes the workflow useful

- faster intake from supplier documentation
- reduced copy-and-paste work
- reviewable results before records are created
- alignment with supplier and manufacturer data already stored in Veviad

## Operational note

Extraction quality can vary by document quality, OCR needs, and data consistency. Treat the workflow as an accelerator, not a substitute for final review.
