## Purpose

The assistant helps locate and interpret permitted workspace data without replacing the source record or report.

![Veviad AI assistant for machinery questions](/images/screenshots/assistant.webp)

## Before you begin

You need assistant permission and an active AI integration. The assistant applies your current data permissions.

## Ask a useful question

1. Open the Veviad AI assistant.
2. State the domain and decision, such as “Which production-critical machines are due for service?”
3. Add relevant limits, dates, locations, or statuses.
4. Review the answer and follow source links.
5. Refine the question rather than assuming missing data means zero.
6. Confirm important values on the source record or in a report.

## Expected result

The conversation records your question and an answer based on available tools and permitted Veviad data.

## Best practices

- Ask one operational question at a time.
- Request source records or exact filters.
- Avoid prompts containing secrets or unnecessary personal data.
- Use Reports for repeatable, scheduled, or distributable analysis.

## Troubleshooting

If the assistant is unavailable, check integration activation and permission. If an answer is incomplete, narrow the question and verify the underlying records.
