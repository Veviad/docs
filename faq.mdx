## Before contacting support

- Confirm the route, workspace, permission, and time of the problem.
- Reproduce with one safe record.
- Capture only the relevant UI; redact personal data and secrets.
- Include a safe error reference when one is shown.
- Ask an administrator to check integration and deployment health when the symptom affects multiple users.

<Note>
  Support never needs your password, SSO secret, SCIM token, license key, or full customer export.
</Note>
