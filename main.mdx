The user manual is the public product documentation for Veviad. It follows the way users work in the application: start from the dashboard, manage assets and related inventory, coordinate people and workflows, configure the workspace, and review reporting or audit information.

## Main areas

| Area | Use it for |
| --- | --- |
| Dashboard | Workspace health, totals, utilization, depreciation, and compliance signals. |
| Asset Management | Asset records, handovers, maintenance, warranties, labels, and lifecycle work. |
| Inventory & Licenses | Software licenses, accessories, and supporting inventory. |
| People & Workflows | Users, assignments, checklists, and recurring workflow automation. |
| Settings & Administration | Organization settings, roles, integrations, SSO, SCIM, and system-level configuration. |
| Analytics & Reporting | Reports, exports, audit logs, and operational traceability. |
| Veviad AI | Assistant workflows and AI-assisted document import. |
| Help & FAQ | Common questions and practical support notes. |

## Best practice

Start with clean master data before importing large asset inventories. Departments, locations, categories, suppliers, manufacturers, status labels, and asset tag rules shape how assets are filtered, reported, assigned, and audited later.
