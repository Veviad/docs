Daily work starts with the customizable dashboard, then moves to the record that needs attention.

## Typical daily loop

1. Scan [Dashboard](/main/dashboard) for inventory, value, maintenance, and checklist signals.
2. Use [Assets](/main/assets) and saved views to work a focused queue.
3. Confirm the responsible [Person](/main/people) and current assignment.
4. Record [Maintenance](/main/maintenance), [Handovers](/main/asset-handover), and [Checklists](/main/checklists) on the asset.
5. Use [Actions](/main/workflows) and [Reports](/analytics/reports) to reduce repetitive review.

## Data ownership

- People own workforce identity and assignments.
- Application users own sign-in authorization.
- Assets own asset checklists, maintenance, handover, invoices, and lifecycle history.
- Reports and audit logs explain trends and changes; they do not replace source records.
