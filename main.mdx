Core Workflows covers the day-to-day modules most teams use after the workspace is configured: Dashboard, Assets, Asset Handover, Maintenance, Licenses, Accessories, People, and Workflows. Use this section for daily asset work and recurring operational processes.

## Highlights

- Dashboard KPIs for assets, utilization, value, and compliance
- Assets, Licenses, and Accessories as three dedicated inventories
- People for HR view, Workflows for checklists and actions
