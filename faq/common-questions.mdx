## Why is a module or button missing?

Your role does not include the required permission, or the feature integration is inactive. Ask an administrator to confirm the intended responsibility.

## Why can I assign an asset to someone who cannot sign in?

People and application access are separate. Assignment-only workforce records are intentional. SCIM People Directory never grants access, roles, credentials, or invitations.

## Why is a person in People but not Admin → Users?

The person has no application entitlement. Grant deliberately local access or include the person in Access SCIM scope if sign-in is required.

## Which SCIM integration should we use?

Use **SCIM People Directory** for workforce profiles and person lifecycle. Use **Access SCIM** for application entitlement and group-derived roles. They share an endpoint but require separate tokens and target populations.

## Do Entra app roles grant Veviad roles?

No. Veviad uses direct Entra group membership and immutable group object IDs only. App roles, group names, SCIM User `roles`, token `roles`, and `wids` do not authorize users.

## Why does authentication show Local — activation pending?

Access SCIM granted application entitlement while SSO is unavailable. The user must accept the activation invitation and create a password. Veviad does not generate a password.

## Can we invite a local user while Access SCIM is active?

Yes, as an explicit local exception. The account remains outside SCIM lifecycle and role authority, so administrators must review and revoke it locally.

## Why are JIT and SSO role synchronization unavailable?

Access SCIM is active and is the sole external authority for access and roles. SSO continues to authenticate. Disabling Access SCIM does not re-enable JIT or role synchronization automatically.

## When do group changes affect roles?

Access SCIM membership and mapping changes reconcile immediately. SSO membership changes apply at the next successful login; SSO mapping changes can reconcile immediately from a valid stored snapshot.

## Why is the role editor disabled?

The user's roles are controlled by SCIM groups or SSO groups. Change the identity-provider membership or Veviad mapping instead.

## Where are Workflows?

The product uses **Actions** for automation. Configure them under Settings → Actions. Asset-only checklist templates are managed separately.

## Can checklists belong to maintenance or people?

No. Current checklist instances belong to assets only. Maintenance has its own records and documents.

## Does the dashboard look the same for everyone?

No. The dashboard layout and configurable widgets are personal to each user.

## Can the asset grid handle 10,000 records?

Yes. It uses server-side filtering, sorting, and lazy row loading. Use saved views or Reports instead of loading or exporting everything.

## Are report AI summaries authoritative?

No. They are grounded assistance attached to a report run. Validate them against rows and chart values.

## Where should I report a problem?

Follow [Troubleshooting and support](/faq/troubleshooting) and use your organization's configured support path.
