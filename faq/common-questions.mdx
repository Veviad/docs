## Why is a module or button missing?

Your role does not include the required permission, or the feature integration is inactive. Ask an administrator to confirm the intended responsibility.

## Why can I assign an asset to someone who cannot sign in?

People and application access are separate. Assignment-only workforce records are intentional.

## Where are Workflows?

The product uses **Actions** for automation. Configure them under Settings → Actions. Asset-only checklist templates are managed separately.

## Can checklists belong to maintenance or people?

No. Current checklist instances belong to assets only. Maintenance has its own records and documents.

## Does the dashboard look the same for everyone?

No. The dashboard layout and configurable widgets are personal to each user.

## Can the asset grid handle 10,000 records?

Yes. It uses server-side filtering, sorting, and lazy row loading. Use saved views or Reports instead of loading or exporting everything.

## Are report AI summaries authoritative?

No. They are grounded assistance attached to a report run. Validate them against rows and chart values.

## Which SCIM endpoint should we use?

Use application-access SCIM for people who should sign in. Use People-directory SCIM for the broader assignable workforce.

## Why is my profile field disabled?

SSO or SCIM may be authoritative for that field. Change it in the identity provider.

## Where should I report a problem?

Follow [Troubleshooting and support](/faq/troubleshooting) and use your organization's configured support path.
