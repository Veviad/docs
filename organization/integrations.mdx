Integrations connect Veviad to identity, AI, document, and delivery services.

![Administration overview for configured integrations](/images/screenshots/admin-integrations.webp)

## Before you begin

You need Admin integration permission and provider values supplied by your organization. Never paste secrets into documentation or support screenshots.

## Identity integrations

| Integration | Purpose | Important boundary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Identity Provider / SSO | Authentication, optional group role synchronization, optional controlled JIT | Access SCIM disables JIT and SSO role sync |
| SCIM People Directory | Workforce profile and person lifecycle | No application access, roles, groups, credentials, or invitations |
| Access SCIM | Application entitlement and group-derived roles | Separate token and population; external Group objects required for roles |

The two SCIM integrations share the tenant SCIM endpoint but use separate bearer tokens. Rotate and scope them independently.

<Info>
  For Entra authorization, use direct memberships and immutable group object IDs. Do not configure app roles or a SCIM User `roles` attribute.
</Info>

## Configure an integration

1. Open **Admin → Integrations**.
2. Select the integration purpose.
3. Enter the required endpoint, identifier, and secret values.
4. Configure role mappings before enabling an authoritative identity flow.
5. Save, activate, and use the built-in connection test where available.
6. Verify the dependent feature with one non-sensitive representative identity or record.
7. Expand scope only after audit results match the expected authority and roles.

## Operational safeguards

- Use dedicated service accounts, tokens, and least-privileged scopes.
- Treat bearer-token rotation as immediate invalidation of the previous token.
- Keep the default identity-provider role at **None** unless approved otherwise.
- Changing SSO provider removes stored SSO membership snapshots; rebuild and retest mappings.
- Disabling Access SCIM does not re-enable JIT or role synchronization automatically.
- Document ownership and renewal dates outside Veviad secret fields.
