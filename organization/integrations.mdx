Integrations connect Veviad to identity, AI, document, and delivery services.

![Administration overview for configured integrations](/images/screenshots/admin-integrations.webp)

## Before you begin

You need Admin integration permission and the provider values supplied by your organization. Never paste secrets into documentation or support screenshots.

## Configure an integration

1. Open **Admin → Integrations**.
2. Select the integration type.
3. Enter the required endpoint, identifier, and secret values.
4. Use the built-in test when required, save the configuration, and activate the integration.
5. Verify the dependent feature with a non-sensitive test record.

## Expected result

The integration shows an active or healthy state and its dependent feature becomes available to authorized users.

## Best practices

- Use dedicated service accounts and scoped credentials.
- Rotate secrets without changing stable identifiers unnecessarily.
- Document ownership and renewal dates outside Veviad secrets fields.

## Troubleshooting

An integration can be configured but inactive, or active but unreachable. Check both state and connectivity, then review the related operator runbook.
